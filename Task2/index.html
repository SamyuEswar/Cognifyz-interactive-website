Cosmic Wonders

Embark on a visual journey through the uncharted territories of the universe, presented in a clean, modern aesthetic.

🌌

The Milky Way

Our galactic home is a barred spiral galaxy with a diameter between 100,000 and 200,000 light-years. It contains an estimated 100-400 billion stars and at least that many planets.

🚀

Martian Rovers

The red planet has captivated humanity for centuries. Modern rovers like Curiosity and Perseverance are currently traversing its dusty, rocky surface, analyzing soil samples.

⚫

Black Holes

These cosmic titans possess gravitational pulls so immense that not even light can escape their event horizon. They are the universe's ultimate mystery, bending spacetime.
